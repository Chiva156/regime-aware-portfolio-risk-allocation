# Results Summary

## Current results

### Regime-aware allocation

The regime-aware inverse-volatility strategy improves risk-adjusted and downside-risk metrics relative to equal-weight allocation.

| Strategy | Ann. Return | Ann. Vol | Sharpe | Sortino | Max DD | CVaR 5% |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Inverse-vol regime | 2.62% | 2.50% | 1.05 | 1.71 | -5.91% | 0.37% |
| Min-var regime | 4.18% | 4.77% | 0.88 | 1.39 | -9.94% | 0.70% |
| Equal weight | 4.53% | 5.38% | 0.84 | 1.34 | -12.19% | 0.80% |

### Interpretation

The current prototype supports the defensive value of regime-aware risk budgeting. The objective is not to claim alpha, but to show improved risk control through adaptive allocation.
